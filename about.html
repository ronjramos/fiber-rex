About us

The company designs, develops, manufactures and sell fiber optic cabling, connectivity, management, systems solutions. It offers a broad range of products directly and through distributors, installers and OEM partners.

Our main products are:

  • FIBER-REX Networking & Telecommunications
  • T-REX Security & Telecommunications
  • LEGRAND Cabling System LCS2
  • DINTEK LAN Cabling Systems

FIBER-REX offers a complete panel to panel solution, utilising all standard connectors and cable formats to deliver a high quality and reliable optical solution. The FIBER-REX solution includes a full range of low fire hazard free and high performance tight buffered and loose tube cables, a comprehensive range of connectors, adaptors, pigtails and patch cords.

T-REX was developed to offer an affordable and inexpensive fiber optic system, for use in FTTX, Security and Telecommunications applications. The product range includes: patch cords, adaptors, pre-terminated cables and MTP/MPO solutions, cables and patch panels.

FIBER-REX and T-REX products are RoHS and REACH Compliant and manufactured to IEC, TIA/EIA and Telcordia specifications within ISO9001 approved facilities.

LEGRAND LCS2 and DINTEK LAN cabling systems offer a diverse array for products ranging from telecommunication cable assemblies to LAN wiring systems and PCB Backplane systems. It provides total solution from technical room to workstation area.

We also offer a “One Stop Shop” approach allowing our customer to consolidate purchase with accessories, active equipment and testing equipment.

Our Business Mission
